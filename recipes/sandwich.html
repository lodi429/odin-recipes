Jerick's Sandwich

Description

Before I start, bring your expectations down all the way past the 7th floor of hell. This is literally the most basic sandwich you can make. I make it all the time though because I like basic and it has some protein in it, and it's kinda filling.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Deli Meat (I like turkey)
  • Cheese (I like pepperjack)
  • Condiments (I like mayo()

Steps

  1. Toast your bread
  2. Put slices of deli meat on toast
  3. Carefully place the cheese on top of the meat
  4. Microwave the sandwich.
  5. Add condiments
  6. IMPORTANT eat
