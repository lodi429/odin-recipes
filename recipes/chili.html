Jerick's Chili


Ingredients

  • 1 Pound Ground Beef
  • 15 Ounces of Diced Tomatoes
  • 15 Ounces of Tomato Sauce
  • 15 Ounces of Dark Red Kidney Beans
  • 1 Onion

Instructions

  1. Dice the onions and saute them in the pot. Saute them until they are brown.
  2. Throw the ground meat in. Cook the meat until browned and then season until it tastes good to you.
  3. Throw all the cans in. Season the pot a little bit, and then let it boil.
  4. Let the pot boil for roughly 15 minutes, and then turn the heat down and let it simmer
  5. Let the pot simmer for minimum 45 minutes. The longer you simmer, the better it tastes. Put it in a slow cooker for maximum simmering ability.
  6. Once you feel it's been simmering for long enough, eat it. Best served with rice, shredded cheese, and sour cream
