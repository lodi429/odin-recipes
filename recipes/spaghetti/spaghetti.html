Simple Spaghetti

Description

This is a very basic spaghetti recipe I pulled off of Google. The image isn't even of the spaghetti, but I have made spaghetti just like this before. Struggle meal. Spaghetti is actually very cost effective without ground beef, fun fact.

Ingredients

  • Box of Spaghetti
  • 1 Jar of Marinara Sauce
  • 1 Pound of Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions
  2. Brown the ground beef, season it to taste while you cook it.
  3. Once beef is finished cooking, add the pasta sauce to the pan.
  4. Cook until the pasta sauce is cooked through, also season that to taste.
  5. Once done with meat sauce, either add all spaghetti to the sauce or just eat it by adding the sauce over the spaghetti. I like to mix the spaghetti and meat sauce together, just like my mom did.
